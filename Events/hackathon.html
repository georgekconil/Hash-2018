#hackathon

Hash Hackathon 2018, aims to provide a platform for student engineers from colleges to show that they have what it takes to challenge the masters of the game. This 22-hour non-stop hackathon will test innovative thinking, design acumen and raw programming skills of students, as well as their mental endurance.

Hackathon Theme is based on :
Health & Social Welfare

Problem Statements will be added soon.

The hackathon comprises the following phases:
1)Pitch Ideas
2)Coding
3)Presentations

Pitch Ideas:
Each team will get 1 min to give an overview of their idea. Jury, mentors, and/or other attendees who are not competing, will express their opinion on its suitability/potential by a show of a red or green card. This is to help teams assess if they are heading in the right direction.

Coding:
This is the 22-hour period where teams will build their application.

Presentations:
This is the final stage where teams will make the final presentation of their application. Each team will get 4 mins to present, followed by 1 min for Q&A from the Jury. A working demonstration of the application (at least of the prototype) will be evaluated. First and Second Prizes worth ₹60,000! Prizes may include internship offers as well! Important: All members from the team will have to register.
