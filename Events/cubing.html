Let's Go

Speed Cubing

In Association With Indian Cube AssociationTM

Date : March 11

Event Description
There will be five individual speed cubing events and for each event a champion will be declared. The individual events are: 3x3 Cubing, 2x2 Cubing, Pyramix Cubing, Blindfolded 3x3 Cubing, One Handed 3x3 Cubing. The results are calculated by taking the average timing of the five attempts to solve the scramble. Each of the five events will consists of 2 rounds. Eight best cubers will be selected for the final round.

Rules and regulations
IDs are compulsory.
Participants are to carry their own puzzle.
Participants will have to strictly follow the game rules specified by ICA at http://indiancubeassociation.in/rules-and-regulation/
A participation certificate will be issued for all the participants of the event. The decisions of judges will be final.
Violation of any rules will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

Registration fee: ₹ 50 per head.

Prizes
₹ 1800 for each of the five events.
