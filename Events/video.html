Let's Go

Video Making

Solo

Date : March 11

Event Description
A two-day challenge wherein the participant gets to shoot videos around the MBCET campus on the first day of HASH and edit the same on the second day to make a video that captures the soul of the fest and the campus. The videos will be collected and kept with the coordinators at the end of the first day. And on the second day, they can bring out their creativity using the edit tools available.

Rules and regulations
College IDs are compulsory.
The participants will have to bring their own laptops.
The SD cards used will have to be formatted and shown to the coordinators at the beginning of the first day.
A participation certificate will be issued for all the participants of the event.
The decisions of judges will be final.
Violation of any rules will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

Registration fee: ₹ 50 per head.
