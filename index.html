This is Hash.

Welcome aboard Hash, the maiden voyage by the CS department of Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology, to revolutionize the way we code. Enjoy the view as we slowly leave the ports of 'Hello World', seranaded by the calms of HTML. Hold on as we meander through the stormy rapids of JS. Plunge into the depths of C and snorkel through the rusted wrecks of ColdFusion. Drink copious amounts of coffee as we cruise around the tropics of Java. Enjoy a five star dinner sprinkled with salty tears of Malbolge developers. Partake in the revelry as the day comes to an end in the murky waters of SyntaxError.
